# McKinsey & Company (2017–2018)

**Business Analyst** · San Francisco, CA

My first job out of Harvard. I advised Fortune 500 clients across pharma, medical devices, software, and retail on revenue strategy — pricing, market sizing, product roadmaps, and launch strategy. Lots of large-scale Excel pricing models, scenario analysis, and roadshows across Asia, Europe, and North America.

## What this chapter taught me

Switching customer domains fast. Four industries in a year meant learning a new market, a new value chain, and a new customer every few months — which turned "ramping on a new domain" from a scary unknown into a known process. It's served me at every job since: consumer at Google, SMB at Square, prosumer-to-enterprise at Webflow.

It's also where I fell in love with the *product* side of strategy — the roadmap and workshop projects were the ones where I didn't want the engagement to end, which is a big part of why I left for product management.
