# International Product Launch Strategy — F500 Pharmaceutical Company

*How do you launch a key product well across dozens of countries at once?*

## The engagement

A Fortune 500 pharmaceutical company was preparing to launch a key product internationally and needed a launch strategy that country teams could actually execute.

## What I did

- **Drafted the launch diagnostic playbook** for the product and identified the **top 3 launch strategies** for prioritization.
- **Led group discussions at 6 launch workshops** for country managers across **Asia, Europe, and North America** — pressure-testing the playbook against each market's realities.
- **Built mathematical models** based on potential trial outcomes and likely customer and competitor responses, so the strategy could flex with how the market actually reacted.

## What stuck with me

A launch strategy is only as good as the country manager's ability to run it on a Tuesday. The workshops taught me to design for the person executing the plan, not the person approving it — a lesson that maps directly to building products: ship for the user in the room, not the stakeholder in the review.
