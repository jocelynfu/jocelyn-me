# 3-Year Product & Technology Roadmap — Online Marketplace

*Where should a major online marketplace play next, and how big is the prize?*

## The engagement

A large online marketplace wanted a 3-year product and technology roadmap: which adjacencies to expand into, which international markets to enter, and what the combined opportunity was worth.

## What I did

- **Sized the opportunity.** Proposed a plan to expand into **3–4 product adjacencies** and **7 key international markets** — increasing SAM by **$100B+** and projected revenue by **$4–5B within 5 years**.
- **Ran product manager workshops** with the client's PM organization to brainstorm and prioritize additional marketplace features — turning the top-down market math into a bottom-up, buildable roadmap.

## What stuck with me

This was the project that pulled me toward product management. The market sizing told us *where* the value was, but the PM workshops were where it got real — features, trade-offs, sequencing. I liked that room better than the boardroom.
