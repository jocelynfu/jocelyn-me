# Travel Photography

Photography is how I actually *see* the places I visit — it slows me down enough to notice things. Travel photography is my main genre: landscapes, streets, and the in-between moments that don't make it into anyone's itinerary.

I also shoot [dog photography](dog.md) and family portraits — turns out the patience you learn waiting for good light transfers directly to waiting for a dog to hold a sit.
