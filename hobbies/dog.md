# Dog

Everything in this repo about work talks about funnels and metrics. This file is about my maltipoo, **Bailey** ([@baileyboo.maltipoo](https://www.instagram.com/baileyboo.maltipoo)), which is frankly the better topic.

## Agility

We train and run **dog agility** — the sport where your dog sprints through tunnels, weaves poles, and takes jumps while you try to keep up and not point in the wrong direction. It's equal parts athletic training, communication, and humility. The dog is rarely the one who makes the mistake.

My ideal morning is outdoors with Bailey — and I almost always end up in a conversation with another dog owner, and almost always learn something.
