# Square Online Growth

*Funnel optimization across the whole seller lifecycle — acquisition, activation, conversion, retention.*

Alongside the [pricing redesign](saas-plan-consolidation.md), I led Square Online's growth & monetization team (15+ engineers) through a steady drumbeat of experiments across the funnel.

## Free trials

The flagship experiment. A free trial is often a user's *first impression of premium* — surface it at the wrong moment and they discount the whole product. We treated it as three design questions: *when* in the journey to surface the trial, auto-enroll vs. self-enroll, and credit card upfront vs. at trial end. Rather than picking answers in a meeting, we benchmarked ~10 competitors, ran usability research, and A/B tested the variants.

The winning combo — surfaced early, self-enrolled, card upfront — drove a **20%+ lift in SaaS conversion**. The surprise: requiring a card *outperformed* the no-obligation variant. A little ownership and urgency at the right moment beats frictionless every time.

## Onboarding & discovery

Redesigned onboarding to cut sign-up steps and launched a "growth hub" for paid-feature discovery — **+20% engagement** with value features.

## Downgrade & churn

Rebuilt the downgrade flow around informed decisions: surfacing each seller's actual feature usage, adding a downgrade survey, and only then experimenting with churn-prevention tactics. Making it easy to leave is part of why people stay.

## Automatic Discounts

Launched Automatic Discounts for sellers — discounts that apply themselves at checkout when conditions are met — driving a **7% lift in seller GPV**.

## Social integrations

Partnered with **Google, Meta, and TikTok** to launch social integrations, which became the top traffic sources for Square Online sellers.
