# Square / Block, Inc. (2021–2024)

**Senior Product Manager → Staff Product Manager, Growth & Monetization** · Remote from Boston

I spent three years at Block working on Square Online (Square's e-commerce product, born from the Weebly acquisition) and later on Square-wide monetization. I joined as a Senior PM leading consumer growth, and was promoted to Staff PM owning growth & monetization for Square Online — leading a team of 15+ engineers — before being selected by the CPO to lead Square's largest-ever pricing & packaging redesign.

## What I did

- **Pricing & packaging.** Redesigned Square Online's SaaS plans end-to-end, then led the Square-wide consolidation of 19 subscription plans into 3 — partnering with finance, legal, and 50+ engineers across 8 teams. → [SaaS plan consolidation](saas-plan-consolidation.md)
- **Platform features.** Launched Customer Accounts, which became the #1 retention driver at Square Online. → [Customer accounts](customer-accounts.md)
- **Funnel growth.** Free trials, onboarding, upgrade/downgrade flows, automatic discounts, and social integrations with Google, Meta, and TikTok. → [Square Online growth](square-online-growth.md)
- **Early gen-AI work.** Shipped AI SEO and image upscaling; drove the proof-of-concept for AI site editing and generation — the work that made me want to go all-in on AI products.

## What this chapter taught me

Good monetization isn't about extracting money — it's about getting users to the outcome they came for. Every durable revenue win at Square came from making the value clearer, not the paywall taller.
