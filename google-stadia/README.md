# Google (2018–2021)

**Associate Product Manager (Google APM program) → Product Manager, Google Stadia** · Mountain View, CA

I joined Google through the APM program, starting on **Google Maps** (Web & App Platforms) — where I launched privacy/location frameworks for Android R and iOS 13, an in-app notifications hub that resolved the top user complaint, and a redesign of the Updates tab (+6% review contributions, +7% user satisfaction). The Maps work was my first taste of a theme that stuck with me: designing systems that motivate people to contribute to something shared — reviews, photos, new places.

Then I moved to **Stadia**, Google's cloud gaming platform, during its 0→1 high-growth phase — and **owned the sharing team**.

## What I did at Stadia

Launched UGC consumer features and B2B2C APIs with 20+ engineers and 5+ game developers, driving **6x MAU growth** and **70% YoY UGC growth**, and onboarded 2 pilot partners with 10+ more in the pipeline.

## What this chapter taught me

Sharing became the throughline of my career: getting one person's creation into the hands of a community, and making it something others can pick up and build on. Maps did it with reviews and photos; Stadia did it with entire playable game moments.
