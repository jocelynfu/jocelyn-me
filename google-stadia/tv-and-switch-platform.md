# TV + Nintendo Switch Platform

*Bringing Stadia beyond the browser and phone.*

## What it was

Stadia's promise was "play anywhere" — and delivering on that meant expanding the platform to the living room and beyond. I worked on extending Stadia's platform to **TV** and **Nintendo Switch** form factors, adapting the experience to new input models, screens, and platform constraints.

This was platform work in the B2B2C sense as well: alongside the consumer surfaces, we launched APIs that let game developers and partners build on Stadia's capabilities — I onboarded 2 pilot partners with 10+ more in the pipeline.

## My role

PM on the platform expansion, working with 20+ engineers and 5+ game developers during Stadia's 0→1 phase.

## What it taught me

Every new platform surface re-tests your product assumptions. A sharing flow designed for a keyboard and mouse means something completely different with a TV remote in hand — the job is figuring out what the product's core promise means on each screen, not porting the UI.
