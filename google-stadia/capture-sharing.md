# Capture Sharing

*Screenshot and video capture of gameplay, shared with other players.*

## What it is

Game-capture sharing let Stadia players capture screenshots and video clips of their gameplay and share them with other players. Table stakes for a gaming platform — but with a cloud twist: since the game rendered in Google's data centers, captures could be high-quality and instant, with no local performance cost to the player.

## My role

As owner of Stadia's sharing team, I led capture sharing from 0 to 1 — the capture pipeline UX, the sharing surfaces, and how captured content flowed between players.

Capture sharing was part of the UGC feature set that drove **70% YoY UGC growth** on the platform.

## How it fits

Capture sharing was the familiar half of Stadia's sharing story — the on-ramp everyone understood. [State Share](state-share.md) was the ambitious half. Together they turned gameplay from a private experience into shareable, community-building content.
